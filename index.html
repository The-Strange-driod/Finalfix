How long does it take to complete a project?
Project completion time depends on the complexity and length. Typically, we deliver standard projects within 5-7 days, urgent projects in 3-5 days, and rush orders within 24-48 hours. We'll provide an exact timeline after reviewing your requirements.
Do you guarantee plagiarism-free work?
Absolutely! All our work is 100% original and plagiarism-free. We provide plagiarism reports upon request and ensure proper citations for all references. We use Turnitin and other professional tools to verify originality before delivery.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
We offer unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and ensure your academic success. Simply request revisions within 7 days of delivery, and we'll make the necessary adjustments at no extra cost.
How do you ensure confidentiality?
Your privacy is our priority. We maintain strict confidentiality, never share your personal information, and all communications are encrypted and secure. We don't store your personal data beyond project completion and follow GDPR-compliant practices.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and other secure payment options convenient for Ghanaian students. Payments are only required after you approve the project outline.
Can you help with urgent deadlines?
Yes! We offer emergency services for urgent deadlines. Rush orders (24-48 hours) are available at a premium rate to ensure quality is not compromised. For extremely urgent requests, contact us directly via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.
Do you provide topic suggestions for free?
Yes! When you submit our project request form, we provide 1-2 relevant topic suggestions at no cost. These are tailored to your field of study and current academic trends. For more extensive topic research, we offer premium consultation services.
What is your pricing structure?
Pricing varies based on project complexity, length, and deadline. Typical rates range from GHS 50-100+ for assignments, GHS 200-300+ for research proposals, and GHS 350-1000+ for full project work. We provide detailed quotes after reviewing your requirements.
How will I receive my completed work?
Completed work is delivered via email or WhatsApp as editable Word documents and PDFs. For presentations, we provide PowerPoint files. We maintain backups for 6 months after delivery.
Can you help with data analysis?
Yes! Our team includes statistics and data analysis experts. We can assist with SPSS, Excel, R, and Python-based analysis. We provide clear interpretation of results and visualization for your project. This service is available as part of full project packages or as a standalone service.
